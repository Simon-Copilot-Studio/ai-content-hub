  ingredients:
    - "Crust: 2 cups graham cracker crumbs, 1/3 cup sugar, 6 tbsp melted butter"
    - "4 packages (32 oz) cream cheese, softened"
    - "1 1/4 cups sugar"
    - "5 large eggs"
    - "2 tsp vanilla extract"
    - "1/4 cup flour"
    - "1 cup sour cream"
    - "Zest of 1 lemon"
  steps:
    - "Press crust mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake at 325°F for 10 minutes."
    - "Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, mixing on low."
    - "Add vanilla, flour, sour cream, and lemon zest. Mix until just combined — don't over-beat."
    - "Pour into crust. Wrap pan bottom in foil and place in a water bath."
    - "Bake at 325°F for 55-65 minutes until edges are set but center jiggles slightly."
    - "Turn off oven, crack door open, and let cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour. Then refrigerate at least 4 hours."
