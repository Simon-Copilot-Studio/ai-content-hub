    - "3 large ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges"
    - "1 English cucumber, cut into chunks"
    - "1/2 red onion, thinly sliced"
    - "1/2 cup Kalamata olives"
    - "1 block (200g) feta cheese"
    - "1 green bell pepper, sliced"
    - "2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil"
    - "1 tbsp red wine vinegar"
    - "1 tsp dried oregano"
    - "Salt to taste"
  steps:
    - "Cut tomatoes into large wedges and cucumber into thick half-moons."
    - "Arrange tomatoes, cucumber, onion, bell pepper, and olives on a plate."
    - "Place the feta block on top — don't crumble it (that's the authentic way)."
    - "Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar. Sprinkle with oregano and salt."
