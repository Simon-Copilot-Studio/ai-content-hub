  category: "Main Course"

  ingredients:
    - "4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)"
    - "1/4 cup soy sauce"
    - "2 tbsp mirin"
    - "2 tbsp sake (or rice wine)"
    - "1 tbsp brown sugar"
    - "1 tsp ginger, grated"
    - "1 clove garlic, minced"
    - "1 tbsp vegetable oil"
    - "Sesame seeds and green onions for garnish"
    - "Steamed rice for serving"
  steps:
    - "Mix soy sauce, mirin, sake, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic for the teriyaki sauce."
    - "Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high. Sear salmon skin-side up 3-4 minutes."
    - "Flip salmon. Pour teriyaki sauce into the pan."
    - "Reduce heat to medium. Baste salmon with sauce as it thickens, about 3-4 minutes."
    - "Salmon is done when it flakes easily. Sauce should be thick and glossy."
    - "Serve over rice. Spoon extra sauce on top. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions."
