  ingredients:
    - "2 cups cooked shredded chicken"
    - "8 flour tortillas"
    - "2 cups salsa verde"
    - "2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese"
    - "1 cup sour cream"
    - "4 oz cream cheese, softened"
    - "1/2 onion, diced"
    - "1 tsp cumin"
    - "Fresh cilantro"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Mix shredded chicken with cream cheese, half the Monterey Jack, cumin, onion, salt, and pepper."
    - "Spread 1/2 cup salsa verde on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish."
    - "Fill each tortilla with chicken mixture. Roll up and place seam-side down."
    - "Pour remaining salsa verde over enchiladas. Top with remaining cheese."
    - "Bake at 375°F for 20-25 minutes until cheese is bubbly."
    - "Drizzle with sour cream and garnish with cilantro."
