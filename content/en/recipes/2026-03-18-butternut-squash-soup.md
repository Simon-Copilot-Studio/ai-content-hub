  ingredients:
    - "1 large butternut squash, peeled and cubed"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "2 cloves garlic"
    - "4 cups vegetable broth"
    - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
    - "2 tbsp butter"
    - "1/4 tsp nutmeg"
    - "Salt and pepper"
    - "Pepitas and sour cream for garnish"
  steps:
    - "Melt butter in a large pot. Sauté onion until golden. Add garlic, cook 1 minute."
    - "Add squash cubes and broth. Bring to a boil, then simmer 25-30 minutes until squash is very tender."
    - "Blend until completely smooth. Stir in cream and nutmeg."
    - "Season to taste. Serve garnished with pepitas and a swirl of sour cream."
