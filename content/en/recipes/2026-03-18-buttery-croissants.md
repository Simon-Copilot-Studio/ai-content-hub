  ingredients:
    - "3 1/2 cups flour"
    - "1/3 cup sugar"
    - "2 1/4 tsp instant yeast"
    - "1 1/3 cups cold butter (for laminating)"
    - "1 cup warm milk"
    - "1 tsp salt"
    - "1 egg + 1 tbsp milk for egg wash"
  steps:
    - "Mix flour, sugar, yeast, salt, and milk into a smooth dough. Refrigerate 1 hour."
    - "Pound cold butter into a flat rectangle between parchment paper."
    - "Roll dough into a large rectangle. Place butter in the center. Fold dough over butter."
    - "Roll out and fold into thirds. Refrigerate 1 hour. Repeat rolling and folding 2 more times."
    - "Roll out final dough to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut into triangles."
    - "Roll each triangle from base to tip. Curve into crescents. Let proof 2 hours until puffy."
    - "Brush with egg wash. Bake at 400°F for 15-18 minutes until deep golden."
