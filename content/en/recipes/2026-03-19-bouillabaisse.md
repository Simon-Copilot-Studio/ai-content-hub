## Why You'll Love This Marseille Bouillabaisse

This French soup is a crowd-pleaser for good reason. With bold flavors, simple preparation, and ingredients you can find at any grocery store, it's the kind of recipe you'll come back to again and again.

**What makes this recipe special:**
- Authentic French flavors with accessible ingredients
- Ready in just 60 minutes from start to finish
- Only 380 calories per serving
- Perfect for cold weather days

## Tips for the Best Marseille Bouillabaisse

1. **Use quality ingredients.** Fresh herbs and good-quality spices make a noticeable difference in the final dish.
2. **Don't rush the cooking.** Let each step develop flavor before moving on.
3. **Taste as you go.** Adjust seasoning to your preference — cooking is personal.
4. **Prep everything first.** Having all ingredients measured and ready (mise en place) makes cooking smoother and more enjoyable.

## Variations

- **Spicier version:** Add extra chili flakes or fresh hot peppers.
- **Milder version:** Reduce spice quantities by half or substitute with mild alternatives.
- **Protein swap:** Try this with chicken, tofu, shrimp, or your preferred protein.

## Serving Suggestions

Serve Marseille Bouillabaisse topped with fresh herbs and a drizzle of olive oil. For a complete meal, pair with a simple side salad and crusty bread. Leftovers taste even better the next day.

## Nutritional Information

Each serving contains approximately 380 calories. This recipe is naturally rich in flavor while being moderate in calories. Adjust portion sizes based on your dietary needs.
