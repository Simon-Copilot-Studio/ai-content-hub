  ingredients:
    - "2 large chicken breasts, halved horizontally"
    - "1 cup breadcrumbs"
    - "1/2 cup grated Parmesan"
    - "2 eggs, beaten"
    - "1/2 cup flour"
    - "1 cup marinara sauce"
    - "1 cup shredded mozzarella"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "1 lb spaghetti"
    - "Fresh basil for garnish"
  steps:
    - "Pound chicken to even 1/2-inch thickness."
    - "Set up breading station: flour, beaten eggs, breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan."
    - "Coat chicken: flour, then egg, then breadcrumb mixture."
    - "Pan-fry in olive oil over medium-high heat, 3-4 minutes per side until golden."
    - "Place chicken in a baking dish. Top with marinara and mozzarella."
    - "Broil 3-4 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden."
    - "Serve over cooked spaghetti with fresh basil."
