  ingredients:
    - "12 lasagna noodles"
    - "1 lb ground beef"
    - "1 lb Italian sausage"
    - "1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce"
    - "2 cups ricotta cheese"
    - "3 cups shredded mozzarella"
    - "1 cup grated Parmesan"
    - "1 egg"
    - "2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Cook lasagna noodles al dente. Brown beef and sausage; drain fat. Stir in marinara sauce."
    - "Mix ricotta with egg, half the Parmesan, basil, salt, and pepper."
    - "Layer in a 9x13 dish: thin layer of meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella. Repeat 3 times."
    - "Top with remaining meat sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan."
    - "Cover with foil. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes. Remove foil, bake 15 more minutes."
    - "Let rest 15 minutes before slicing — this is essential for clean cuts."
