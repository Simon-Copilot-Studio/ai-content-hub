## Why You'll Love This Filipino Chicken Adobo

This Filipino main course is a crowd-pleaser for good reason. With bold flavors, simple preparation, and ingredients you can find at any grocery store, it's the kind of recipe you'll come back to again and again.

**What makes this recipe special:**
- Authentic Filipino flavors with accessible ingredients
- Ready in just 45 minutes from start to finish
- Only 380 calories per serving
- Perfect for meal prep

## Tips for the Best Filipino Chicken Adobo

1. **Use quality ingredients.** Fresh herbs and good-quality spices make a noticeable difference in the final dish.
2. **Don't rush the cooking.** Let each step develop flavor before moving on.
3. **Taste as you go.** Adjust seasoning to your preference — cooking is personal.
4. **Prep everything first.** Having all ingredients measured and ready (mise en place) makes cooking smoother and more enjoyable.

## Variations

- **Spicier version:** Add extra chili flakes or fresh hot peppers.
- **Milder version:** Reduce spice quantities by half or substitute with mild alternatives.
- **Protein swap:** Try this with chicken, tofu, shrimp, or your preferred protein.

## Serving Suggestions

Serve Filipino Chicken Adobo over a bed of fluffy couscous or mashed potatoes. For a complete meal, pair with a simple side salad and crusty bread. Leftovers taste even better the next day.

Each serving contains approximately 380 calories. This recipe is naturally rich in flavor while being balanced in macronutrients. Adjust portion sizes based on your dietary needs.
