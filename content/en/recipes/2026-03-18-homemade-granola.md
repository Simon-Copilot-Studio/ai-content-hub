Once you make homemade granola, you'll never go back to store-bought. It's cheaper, healthier, and infinitely customizable. Plus, your kitchen will smell incredible.

## The Cluster Secret

The #1 question about granola: how do I get those perfect clusters? Two things:

1. **Press it down** — Before baking, use a spatula to firmly compact the mixture on the baking sheet
2. **Don't touch it** — No stirring during baking, no breaking apart while cooling. Let it set completely first

## Customization Ideas

- **Tropical**: Coconut flakes, macadamia nuts, dried mango
- **Chocolate**: Add cocoa powder and chocolate chips after baking
- **Nut-free**: Use extra seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, hemp)
- **Protein boost**: Add protein powder to the dry mix

## Serving Suggestions

- With cold milk like cereal
- Over yogurt parfaits with fresh fruit
- As a topping for smoothie bowls
- Straight from the jar as a snack
