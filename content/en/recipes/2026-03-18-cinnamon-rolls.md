    - "Dough: 4 cups flour, 1/3 cup sugar, 2 1/4 tsp yeast, 1 cup warm milk, 1/3 cup melted butter, 1 egg, 1 tsp salt"
    - "Filling: 1/2 cup brown sugar, 2 tbsp cinnamon, 1/3 cup softened butter"
    - "Frosting: 4 oz cream cheese, 2 cups powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk, 1 tsp vanilla"
  steps:
    - "Make dough: combine ingredients, knead 8 minutes until smooth. Let rise 1 hour."
    - "Roll dough into a large rectangle (about 16x12 inches). Spread softened butter over surface."
    - "Sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon mixture evenly over butter."
    - "Roll up tightly from the long side. Cut into 12 equal pieces."
    - "Place rolls in a greased 9x13 pan. Cover and let rise 45 minutes."
    - "Bake at 375°F for 22-25 minutes until golden."
    - "Make frosting: beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla. Spread over warm rolls."
