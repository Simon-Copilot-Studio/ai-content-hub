  ingredients:
    - "2 cups flour"
    - "1/3 cup sugar"
    - "1 tbsp baking powder"
    - "1/4 tsp salt"
    - "1/3 cup cold butter, cubed"
    - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
    - "1 egg"
    - "1 tsp vanilla"
    - "Clotted cream and jam for serving"
  steps:
    - "Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Cut in cold butter until pea-sized pieces form."
    - "Stir in cream, egg, and vanilla until dough just comes together."
    - "Pat dough to 1-inch thickness on a floured surface. Cut with a 2.5-inch round cutter."
    - "Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Brush tops with cream."
    - "Bake at 400°F for 12-15 minutes until golden."
    - "Serve warm with clotted cream and your favorite jam."
