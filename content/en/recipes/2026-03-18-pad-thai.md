  ingredients:
    - "8 oz flat rice noodles"
    - "1/2 lb shrimp, peeled"
    - "2 eggs"
    - "1 cup bean sprouts"
    - "3 green onions, chopped"
    - "1/4 cup roasted peanuts, crushed"
    - "Sauce: 3 tbsp fish sauce, 2 tbsp tamarind paste, 2 tbsp brown sugar, 1 tbsp lime juice"
    - "2 tbsp vegetable oil"
    - "Lime wedges and cilantro for serving"
  steps:
    - "Soak rice noodles in hot water 8-10 minutes until pliable. Drain."
    - "Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl."
    - "Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Cook shrimp 2 minutes until pink. Remove."
    - "Crack eggs into the wok. Scramble quickly."
    - "Add noodles and sauce. Toss until noodles absorb the sauce, about 2 minutes."
    - "Return shrimp. Add bean sprouts and green onions. Toss to combine."
    - "Serve topped with crushed peanuts, lime wedges, and cilantro."
