    - "3 cups bread flour"
    - "1 1/2 tsp salt"
    - "1/2 tsp instant yeast"
    - "1 1/2 cups warm water"
  steps:
    - "Stir flour, salt, yeast, and water together until a shaggy dough forms. No kneading needed."
    - "Cover with plastic wrap and let rise at room temperature for 12-18 hours."
    - "Turn dough onto a floured surface. Fold it over itself a few times. Shape into a round loaf."
    - "Place on parchment paper, cover, and let rise 1 hour."
    - "Place a Dutch oven in the oven and preheat to 450°F for 30 minutes."
    - "Carefully lower the dough (on parchment) into the hot Dutch oven. Cover with lid."
    - "Bake covered 30 minutes. Remove lid and bake 12-15 minutes more until deep golden."
