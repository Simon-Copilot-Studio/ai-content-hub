## Pro Tips for Perfect Results

The secret to great Filipino Bibingka lies in a few key techniques:

1. **Temperature matters.** Make sure your pan or oven is properly preheated before you start cooking.
2. **Season in layers.** Add a little salt at each stage rather than all at once at the end.
3. **Let it rest.** If applicable, allow the dish to rest for a few minutes before serving — this lets flavors settle.

## How to Store and Reheat

- **Refrigerator:** Up to 3-4 days in an airtight container.
- **Freezer:** Up to 3 months. Thaw overnight in the fridge.
- **Reheating:** Warm gently on the stove or in the microwave. Add a splash of water or broth to prevent drying out.
