  ingredients:
    - "1 lb ground pork"
    - "2 cups napa cabbage, finely chopped and squeezed dry"
    - "3 green onions, minced"
    - "2 tbsp soy sauce"
    - "1 tbsp sesame oil"
    - "1 tbsp ginger, grated"
    - "40 dumpling wrappers"
    - "Dipping sauce: 3 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tsp chili oil, sesame seeds"
  steps:
    - "Mix pork, cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, and ginger."
    - "Place 1 tablespoon filling in the center of each wrapper. Fold and pleat to seal."
    - "Heat oil in a non-stick skillet. Place dumplings flat-side down."
    - "Cook 2 minutes until bottoms are golden and crispy."
    - "Add 1/3 cup water. Cover immediately. Steam 5-6 minutes until water evaporates."
    - "Serve crispy-side up with dipping sauce."
