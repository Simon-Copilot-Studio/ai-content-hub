    - "2 cups flour"
    - "2 cups sugar"
    - "3/4 cup cocoa powder"
    - "2 tsp baking soda"
    - "1 tsp salt"
    - "2 eggs"
    - "1 cup buttermilk"
    - "1 cup hot coffee"
    - "1/2 cup vegetable oil"
    - "2 tsp vanilla"
    - "Buttercream: 1 cup butter, 4 cups powdered sugar, 1/2 cup cocoa, 1/4 cup cream"
  steps:
    - "Whisk flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl."
    - "Add eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Beat until combined."
    - "Stir in hot coffee — the batter will be thin. This is correct and creates an incredibly moist cake."
    - "Pour into two greased 9-inch round pans. Bake at 350°F for 30-35 minutes."
    - "Cool completely before frosting."
    - "Make buttercream: beat butter until fluffy, add powdered sugar, cocoa, cream, and vanilla. Beat until smooth and spreadable."
    - "Frost between layers and on top. Decorate as desired."
