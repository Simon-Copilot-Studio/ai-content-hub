  ingredients:
    - "2 cups heavy cream"
    - "1/2 cup sugar"
    - "1 tsp vanilla extract"
    - "2 1/4 tsp gelatin powder"
    - "3 tbsp cold water"
    - "Berry sauce: 2 cups mixed berries, 3 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp lemon juice"
  steps:
    - "Sprinkle gelatin over cold water. Let bloom for 5 minutes."
    - "Heat cream and sugar in a saucepan until sugar dissolves. Don't boil."
    - "Remove from heat. Stir in bloomed gelatin until completely dissolved. Add vanilla."
    - "Pour into ramekins or glasses. Refrigerate at least 4 hours until set."
    - "Make berry sauce: cook berries with sugar and lemon juice for 5 minutes. Lightly mash."
    - "Serve panna cotta with berry sauce drizzled on top."
