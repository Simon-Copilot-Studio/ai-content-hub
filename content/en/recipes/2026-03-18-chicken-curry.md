  category: "Main Course"
  cuisine: "Indian"
  ingredients:
    - "1.5 lbs chicken thighs, boneless, cut into chunks"
    - "1 large onion, finely diced"
    - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1 tbsp ginger, grated"
    - "1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes"
    - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
    - "2 tbsp butter or ghee"
    - "1 tbsp garam masala"
    - "1 tsp turmeric"
    - "1 tsp cumin"
    - "1 tsp chili powder"
    - "Fresh cilantro for garnish"
    - "Basmati rice for serving"
  steps:
    - "Heat butter in a large skillet. Cook chicken until golden brown. Remove and set aside."
    - "Sauté onion until soft and golden, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute."
    - "Add garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder. Toast spices 30 seconds."
    - "Pour in crushed tomatoes. Simmer 10 minutes until sauce thickens."
    - "Return chicken to pan. Cover and simmer 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through."
    - "Stir in heavy cream. Garnish with cilantro. Serve over basmati rice."
