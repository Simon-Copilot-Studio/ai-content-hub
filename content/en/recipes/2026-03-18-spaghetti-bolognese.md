  ingredients:
    - "1 lb spaghetti"
    - "1 lb ground beef"
    - "1/2 lb ground pork"
    - "1 onion, finely diced"
    - "2 carrots, finely diced"
    - "2 celery stalks, finely diced"
    - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes"
    - "1/2 cup red wine"
    - "2 tbsp tomato paste"
    - "1/2 cup whole milk"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "Parmesan cheese for serving"
  steps:
    - "Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven. Sauté onion, carrot, and celery until softened, about 8 minutes."
    - "Add ground beef and pork. Cook until browned, breaking into small pieces."
    - "Stir in garlic and tomato paste, cook 2 minutes until fragrant."
    - "Pour in red wine and let it reduce by half."
    - "Add crushed tomatoes, season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer."
    - "Reduce heat to low, partially cover, and simmer for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Stir in milk during the last 30 minutes."
    - "Cook spaghetti al dente. Serve sauce over pasta with freshly grated Parmesan."
