  ingredients:
    - "2 1/2 cups flour"
    - "1 cup sugar"
    - "2 tsp baking powder"
    - "1/2 tsp salt"
    - "2 eggs"
    - "1 cup sour cream"
    - "1/2 cup melted butter"
    - "1 tsp vanilla"
    - "1 1/2 cups blueberries"
    - "Coarse sugar for topping"
  steps:
    - "Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt."
    - "In another bowl, mix eggs, sour cream, melted butter, and vanilla."
    - "Fold wet into dry until just combined. Gently fold in blueberries."
    - "Divide batter among 12 muffin cups (fill to the top for domed muffins). Sprinkle with coarse sugar."
    - "Bake at 400°F for 5 minutes, then reduce to 350°F for 18-20 minutes."
    - "Cool in pan 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack."
