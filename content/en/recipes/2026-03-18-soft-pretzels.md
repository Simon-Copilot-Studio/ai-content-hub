  ingredients:
    - "3 cups flour"
    - "1 tbsp sugar"
    - "2 1/4 tsp instant yeast"
    - "1 cup warm water"
    - "2 tbsp melted butter"
    - "1 tsp salt"
    - "Baking soda bath: 10 cups water + 2/3 cup baking soda"
    - "Coarse salt"
    - "Melted butter for brushing"
  steps:
    - "Mix flour, sugar, yeast, water, butter, and salt. Knead 5 minutes. Let rise 1 hour."
    - "Divide dough into 8 pieces. Roll each into a 24-inch rope."
    - "Shape into pretzels: make a U-shape, cross the ends twice, fold down to the bottom of the U."
    - "Bring baking soda water to a boil. Boil each pretzel 30 seconds."
    - "Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Sprinkle with coarse salt."
    - "Bake at 425°F for 12-14 minutes until deep golden. Brush with melted butter."
