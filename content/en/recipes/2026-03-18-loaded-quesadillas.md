    - "2 large flour tortillas"
    - "1 cup cooked shredded chicken"
    - "1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend"
    - "1/2 bell pepper, sliced"
    - "1/4 onion, sliced"
    - "Butter for crisping"
    - "Sour cream, guacamole, and salsa for serving"
  steps:
    - "Sauté bell pepper and onion until softened."
    - "Butter one side of each tortilla."
    - "Place tortilla butter-side down in a skillet. Layer cheese, chicken, peppers, onions, and more cheese."
    - "Top with second tortilla, butter-side up."
    - "Cook over medium heat 3-4 minutes per side until golden and cheese is melted."
    - "Cut into wedges. Serve with sour cream, guacamole, and salsa."
