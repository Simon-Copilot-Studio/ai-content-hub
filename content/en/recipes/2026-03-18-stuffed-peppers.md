  ingredients:
    - "6 bell peppers (mixed colors), tops cut off and seeded"
    - "1 lb ground beef"
    - "1 cup cooked rice"
    - "1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1 cup shredded cheddar cheese"
    - "1 tsp Italian seasoning"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Brown ground beef with onion and garlic. Drain fat."
    - "Stir in cooked rice, half the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper."
    - "Stuff peppers with the beef-rice mixture."
    - "Place in a baking dish. Pour remaining tomato sauce over and around peppers."
    - "Cover with foil. Bake at 375°F for 30 minutes."
    - "Remove foil, top with cheese. Bake 5 more minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly."
