  ingredients:
    - "Crust: 1 1/4 cups flour, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 1/2 cup cold butter, 1 egg yolk"
    - "Pastry cream: 2 cups milk, 4 egg yolks, 1/2 cup sugar, 3 tbsp cornstarch, 2 tbsp butter, 1 tsp vanilla"
    - "Fresh fruits: strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, raspberries"
    - "1/4 cup apricot jam (for glaze)"
  steps:
    - "Make crust: pulse flour, sugar, and butter. Add egg yolk. Press into a tart pan. Bake at 375°F for 15-18 minutes."
    - "Make pastry cream: heat milk. Whisk yolks, sugar, and cornstarch. Temper with hot milk, return to pan, cook until thick. Stir in butter and vanilla. Chill."
    - "Spread pastry cream into cooled tart shell."
    - "Arrange fresh fruits decoratively on top."
    - "Warm apricot jam and brush over fruit for a glossy finish."
