  ingredients:
    - "1 lb ground beef (80/20)"
    - "4 brioche buns"
    - "4 slices American cheese"
    - "1 onion, thinly sliced"
    - "Pickles"
    - "Special sauce: 2 tbsp mayo, 1 tbsp ketchup, 1 tbsp relish, 1 tsp mustard"
    - "Salt and pepper"
    - "Butter for buns"
  steps:
    - "Divide beef into 4 balls. Season generously with salt and pepper."
    - "Heat a cast iron skillet over high heat until smoking."
    - "Place beef balls on skillet. Immediately smash flat with a heavy spatula. Cook 2-3 minutes until edges are deeply crispy."
    - "Flip, add cheese. Cook 1 more minute."
    - "Toast buttered buns in the same skillet."
    - "Assemble: sauce on both buns, patty with cheese, pickles, onions."
