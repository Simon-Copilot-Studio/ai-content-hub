    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Heat olive oil in a large pot. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant."
    - "Add carrots and green beans. Cook 3 minutes."
    - "Add zucchini, tomatoes, corn, and broth. Bring to a boil."
    - "Simmer 15-20 minutes until all vegetables are tender."
    - "Season with salt, pepper, and fresh parsley."
