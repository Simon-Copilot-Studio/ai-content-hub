  ingredients:
    - "4 cod fillets"
    - "1 cup flour"
    - "1 cup beer (lager)"
    - "1 tsp baking powder"
    - "4 large russet potatoes, cut into thick chips"
    - "Vegetable oil for frying"
    - "Malt vinegar"
    - "Lemon wedges"
    - "Salt"
  steps:
    - "Cut potatoes into thick chips. Soak in cold water 30 minutes. Drain and pat dry."
    - "Fry chips at 325°F for 5 minutes until soft but not colored. Set aside."
    - "Make batter: whisk flour, baking powder, salt, and beer until smooth."
    - "Heat oil to 375°F. Dip fish in flour, then batter. Fry 5-6 minutes until golden."
    - "Refry chips at 375°F for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy."
    - "Drain everything on paper towels. Season with salt. Serve with malt vinegar and lemon."
