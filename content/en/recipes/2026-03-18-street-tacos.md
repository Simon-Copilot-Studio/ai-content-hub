  ingredients:
    - "1 lb flank steak (or al pastor pork)"
    - "8 small corn tortillas"
    - "1/2 white onion, finely diced"
    - "1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped"
    - "2 limes"
    - "1 tsp cumin"
    - "1 tsp chili powder"
    - "1 tsp garlic powder"
    - "Salsa verde or roja"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Season steak with cumin, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper."
    - "Grill or sear over high heat, 3-4 minutes per side for medium. Rest 5 minutes, then slice against the grain."
    - "Warm tortillas on a dry skillet or directly over a gas flame."
    - "Top each tortilla with sliced steak, diced onion, cilantro, a squeeze of lime, and salsa."
    - "Serve two tortillas stacked per taco for authenticity."
