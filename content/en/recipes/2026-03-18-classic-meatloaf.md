  ingredients:
    - "2 lbs ground beef (80/20)"
    - "1 cup breadcrumbs"
    - "2 eggs"
    - "1/2 cup milk"
    - "1 onion, finely diced"
    - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1/4 cup ketchup (for glaze)"
    - "2 tbsp brown sugar"
    - "1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce"
    - "1 tsp dried thyme"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Mix ground beef, breadcrumbs, eggs, milk, onion, garlic, Worcestershire, thyme, salt, and pepper. Don't overmix."
    - "Shape into a loaf on a parchment-lined baking sheet (or press into a loaf pan)."
    - "Mix ketchup and brown sugar for the glaze."
    - "Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes. Brush with glaze."
    - "Continue baking 15-20 minutes until internal temp reaches 160°F."
    - "Rest 10 minutes before slicing."
