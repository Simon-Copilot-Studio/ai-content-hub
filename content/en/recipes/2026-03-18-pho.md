  ingredients:
    - "Broth: 3 lbs beef bones, 1 lb beef brisket, 1 onion (halved), 3-inch ginger (halved), 3 star anise, 4 cloves, 1 cinnamon stick, 2 tbsp fish sauce"
    - "14 oz flat rice noodles"
    - "1/2 lb eye of round beef, thinly sliced"
    - "Toppings: bean sprouts, Thai basil, lime, jalapeño, hoisin sauce, sriracha"
  steps:
    - "Char onion and ginger directly over a gas flame or under a broiler until blackened."
    - "Blanch beef bones. Clean pot, return bones with fresh water. Add charred onion, ginger, and spices."
    - "Simmer gently (never boil) for at least 3 hours. Add brisket after 1 hour."
    - "Strain broth. Season with fish sauce and salt."
    - "Cook rice noodles according to package."
    - "Slice brisket. Arrange noodles, brisket, and raw beef slices in bowls."
    - "Ladle boiling hot broth over (it cooks the raw beef). Serve with all the toppings."
