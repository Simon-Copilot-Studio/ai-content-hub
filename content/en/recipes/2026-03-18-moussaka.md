  ingredients:
    - "2 large eggplants, sliced 1/4-inch thick"
    - "1 lb ground lamb or beef"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "3 cloves garlic"
    - "1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes"
    - "1/2 tsp cinnamon"
    - "Béchamel: 4 tbsp butter, 4 tbsp flour, 2 cups milk, 1/4 tsp nutmeg, 1 egg yolk"
    - "1/2 cup grated Parmesan"
    - "Olive oil, salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Salt eggplant slices and let sit 30 minutes. Pat dry and brush with olive oil. Broil until golden."
    - "Brown meat with onion and garlic. Add tomatoes, cinnamon, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes."
    - "Make béchamel: melt butter, whisk in flour, add milk slowly. Cook until thick. Off heat, stir in nutmeg, Parmesan, and egg yolk."
    - "Layer in a 9x13 dish: eggplant, meat sauce, eggplant, meat sauce, béchamel on top."
    - "Bake at 375°F for 45-50 minutes until golden and bubbly."
    - "Rest 15 minutes before cutting."
