  ingredients:
    - "2 cans (6.5 oz) chopped clams with juice"
    - "4 strips bacon, diced"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "2 stalks celery, diced"
    - "3 medium potatoes, cubed"
    - "2 cups clam juice or fish stock"
    - "1 cup heavy cream"
    - "2 tbsp flour"
    - "2 tbsp butter"
    - "Fresh thyme"
    - "Oyster crackers for serving"
  steps:
    - "Cook bacon in a Dutch oven until crispy. Remove bacon, keep the fat."
    - "Sauté onion and celery in bacon fat until softened."
    - "Add butter and flour. Stir 1 minute to make a roux."
    - "Pour in clam juice and add potatoes. Simmer 15 minutes until potatoes are tender."
    - "Add clams and heavy cream. Heat through gently — don't boil."
    - "Serve topped with crispy bacon, thyme, and oyster crackers."
