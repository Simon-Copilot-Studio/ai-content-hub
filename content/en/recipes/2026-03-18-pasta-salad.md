    - "1 lb rotini pasta"

    - "1 cup diced mozzarella"
    - "1/2 cup sliced black olives"
    - "1/2 bell pepper, diced"
    - "1/4 cup pepperoncini, sliced"
    - "1/2 cup Italian dressing"
    - "2 tbsp fresh basil, chopped"
    - "Salt and pepper to taste"
  steps:
    - "Cook pasta al dente according to package directions. Rinse under cold water and drain well."
    - "Combine pasta with tomatoes, salami, mozzarella, olives, bell pepper, and pepperoncini."
    - "Pour Italian dressing over and toss until evenly coated."
    - "Season to taste, garnish with fresh basil. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving — flavors develop in the fridge."
