  ingredients:
    - "3 cups bread flour"
    - "2 1/4 tsp instant yeast"
    - "1 1/2 tsp salt"
    - "1 1/4 cups warm water"
    - "4 tbsp olive oil (plus more for drizzling)"
    - "Fresh rosemary"
    - "Flaky sea salt"
  steps:
    - "Mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and 2 tbsp olive oil. Stir until a shaggy dough forms."
    - "Pour 2 tbsp olive oil into a 9x13 pan. Transfer dough to pan. Cover and let rise 2 hours."
    - "With oiled fingers, press dimples all over the dough."
    - "Drizzle generously with olive oil. Press rosemary sprigs and flaky salt into the surface."
    - "Bake at 425°F for 22-25 minutes until golden brown."
    - "Cool slightly, slice, and serve warm."
