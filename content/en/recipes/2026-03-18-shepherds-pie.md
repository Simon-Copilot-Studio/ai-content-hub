  ingredients:
    - "1.5 lbs ground lamb (or beef for cottage pie)"
    - "2 lbs potatoes, peeled and cubed"
    - "1 cup frozen peas and carrots"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "2 tbsp tomato paste"
    - "1 cup beef broth"
    - "1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce"
    - "1/2 cup butter"
    - "1/2 cup milk"
    - "1 cup shredded cheddar cheese"
  steps:
    - "Boil potatoes until tender. Mash with butter, milk, salt, and pepper. Set aside."
    - "Brown lamb in a skillet. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened."
    - "Stir in tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, and broth. Add peas and carrots. Simmer 10 minutes."
    - "Transfer meat mixture to a baking dish. Spread mashed potatoes evenly on top. Score with a fork."
    - "Sprinkle with cheddar cheese."
    - "Bake at 400°F for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbly."
