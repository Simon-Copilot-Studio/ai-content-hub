  ingredients:
    - "1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined"
    - "12 oz linguine"
    - "4 tbsp butter"
    - "3 tbsp olive oil"
    - "6 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1/2 cup dry white wine"
    - "Juice of 1 lemon"
    - "1/4 tsp red pepper flakes"
    - "1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped"
    - "Parmesan cheese for serving"
  steps:
    - "Cook linguine al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water."
    - "Heat olive oil and butter in a large skillet. Sauté garlic and red pepper flakes 30 seconds."
    - "Add shrimp in a single layer. Cook 1-2 minutes per side until pink."
    - "Pour in white wine and lemon juice. Simmer 2 minutes."
    - "Toss in cooked linguine with a splash of pasta water. Toss until coated."
    - "Garnish with parsley and Parmesan."
