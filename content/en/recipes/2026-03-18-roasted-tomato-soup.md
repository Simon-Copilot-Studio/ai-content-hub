  ingredients:
    - "3 lbs Roma tomatoes, halved"
    - "1 onion, quartered"
    - "6 cloves garlic"
    - "3 tbsp olive oil"
    - "2 cups vegetable broth"
    - "1/4 cup fresh basil"
    - "1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)"
    - "Salt and pepper"
    - "Grilled cheese for serving"
  steps:
    - "Toss tomatoes, onion, and garlic with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a baking sheet."
    - "Roast at 400°F for 30-35 minutes until charred and caramelized."
    - "Transfer roasted vegetables to a pot. Add broth and basil. Simmer 10 minutes."
    - "Blend until smooth with an immersion blender."
    - "Stir in cream if using. Adjust seasoning. Serve with grilled cheese."
