  ingredients:
    - "12 lasagna noodles"
    - "2 cups ricotta cheese"
    - "3 cups shredded mozzarella"
    - "10 oz frozen spinach, thawed and squeezed dry"
    - "2 cups sliced mushrooms"
    - "2 zucchini, thinly sliced"
    - "1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce"
    - "1 egg"
    - "1/2 cup grated Parmesan"
    - "Italian seasoning, salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Cook noodles al dente. Sauté mushrooms and zucchini until golden."
    - "Mix ricotta, egg, half the Parmesan, spinach, and seasonings."
    - "Layer in a 9x13 dish: sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, sautéed vegetables, mozzarella. Repeat."
    - "Top with remaining sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan."
    - "Cover with foil. Bake at 375°F for 40 minutes. Remove foil, bake 10 more minutes."
    - "Rest 15 minutes before serving."
