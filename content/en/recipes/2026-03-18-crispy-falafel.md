  ingredients:
    - "2 cans (15 oz each) chickpeas, drained"
    - "1 cup fresh parsley"
    - "1/2 cup fresh cilantro"
    - "1 small onion, quartered"
    - "4 cloves garlic"
    - "2 tbsp flour"
    - "1 tsp cumin"
    - "1 tsp coriander"
    - "1/2 tsp cayenne"
    - "Salt and pepper"
    - "Pita bread, tahini sauce, pickled turnips for serving"
  steps:
    - "Pulse chickpeas, parsley, cilantro, onion, and garlic in a food processor until finely chopped but not pureed."
    - "Mix in flour, cumin, coriander, cayenne, salt, and pepper."
    - "Refrigerate mixture 30 minutes."
    - "Form into balls and flatten slightly. Place on a greased baking sheet."
    - "Brush with olive oil. Bake at 400°F for 12 minutes per side until golden and crispy."
    - "Serve in warm pita with tahini sauce, tomatoes, cucumber, and pickled turnips."
