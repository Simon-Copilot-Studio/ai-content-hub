  ingredients:
    - "1 can (14 oz) coconut milk"
    - "2 tbsp Thai green curry paste"
    - "2 cups mixed vegetables (bell pepper, zucchini, eggplant, bamboo shoots)"
    - "1 cup broccoli florets"
    - "1 tbsp fish sauce (or soy sauce for vegan)"
    - "1 tbsp brown sugar"
    - "Fresh Thai basil"
    - "Jasmine rice for serving"
    - "1 Thai chili, sliced (optional)"
  steps:
    - "Heat 2 tbsp coconut cream (thick part from top of can) in a wok. Fry curry paste 1 minute until fragrant."
    - "Add remaining coconut milk. Bring to a simmer."
    - "Add harder vegetables first (broccoli, eggplant). Cook 5 minutes."
    - "Add softer vegetables (bell pepper, zucchini, bamboo shoots). Cook 5 more minutes."
    - "Season with fish sauce and brown sugar. Adjust to taste."
    - "Top with Thai basil and chili. Serve over jasmine rice."
