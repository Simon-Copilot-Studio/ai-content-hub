The breakfast burrito is the ultimate grab-and-go morning meal. This version packs in everything you love about breakfast — eggs, bacon, crispy potatoes, and cheese — all wrapped in a warm tortilla.

## Assembly Order Matters

Layer strategically: cheese goes on first (closest to the warm tortilla so it melts), then hot ingredients, then cold toppings like avocado and sour cream on top.

## The Perfect Roll

1. Place filling in a line across the center, leaving 2 inches on each side
2. Fold the bottom edge up and over the filling
3. Tuck in the left and right sides
4. Roll away from you, keeping it tight

## Meal Prep Champion

These burritos freeze incredibly well. Make a batch of 10 on Sunday, and you've got breakfast handled for two weeks. The key is wrapping tightly in foil before freezing.

## Variations

- **Vegetarian**: Replace bacon with black beans and sautéed peppers
- **Keto**: Use a low-carb tortilla and skip the hash browns
- **Southwest**: Add corn, black beans, and chipotle sauce
