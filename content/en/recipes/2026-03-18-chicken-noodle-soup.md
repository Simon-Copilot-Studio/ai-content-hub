  ingredients:
    - "1 whole chicken (about 4 lbs) or 2 lbs bone-in chicken"
    - "8 cups water"
    - "3 carrots, sliced"
    - "3 celery stalks, sliced"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "8 oz egg noodles"
    - "2 bay leaves"
    - "Fresh dill and parsley"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Place chicken in a large pot with water, bay leaves, salt. Bring to a boil, then simmer 30-40 minutes."
    - "Remove chicken. Shred the meat, discard bones and skin. Strain broth."
    - "Return broth to pot. Add carrots, celery, onion, and garlic. Simmer 10 minutes."
    - "Add egg noodles and shredded chicken. Cook until noodles are tender, about 8 minutes."
    - "Season with salt, pepper, and fresh herbs."
