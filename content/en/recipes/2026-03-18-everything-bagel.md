    - "1 tbsp barley malt syrup (for boiling water)"

  steps:
    - "Mix flour, yeast, salt, sugar, and water. Knead 10 minutes until smooth and elastic."
    - "Divide into 8 pieces. Shape each into a ball, poke a hole through the center, and stretch to form a ring."
    - "Let rise 1 hour. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of water with malt syrup to a boil."
    - "Boil each bagel 1 minute per side. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet."
    - "Brush with egg wash, sprinkle generously with everything seasoning."
    - "Bake at 425°F for 20-25 minutes until deep golden brown."
