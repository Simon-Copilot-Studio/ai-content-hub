  ingredients:
    - "1 cup quinoa, rinsed"
    - "1 English cucumber, diced"
    - "1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved"
    - "1/2 cup Kalamata olives, halved"
    - "1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese"
    - "1/4 cup red onion, finely diced"
    - "1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped"
    - "3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil"
    - "2 tbsp lemon juice"
    - "1 clove garlic, minced"
    - "Salt and pepper to taste"
  steps:
    - "Cook quinoa according to package directions. Fluff with a fork and let cool."
    - "Combine cucumber, tomatoes, olives, red onion, and parsley in a large bowl."
    - "Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper for the dressing."
    - "Add cooled quinoa to the vegetables. Pour dressing over and toss well."
    - "Top with crumbled feta. Serve immediately or refrigerate — it gets better overnight."
