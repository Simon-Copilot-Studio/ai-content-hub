  category: "Main Course"
  cuisine: "Chinese"
  ingredients:
    - "4 cups day-old cooked rice (cold from fridge)"
    - "3 eggs, beaten"
    - "3 green onions, chopped"
    - "2 tbsp soy sauce"
    - "1 tbsp sesame oil"
    - "2 tbsp vegetable oil"
    - "1 cup frozen peas and carrots"
    - "Salt and white pepper"
  steps:
    - "Heat vegetable oil in a wok over the highest heat until smoking."
    - "Add beaten eggs. Scramble quickly for 30 seconds. Remove and set aside."
    - "Add more oil if needed. Toss in rice, breaking up any clumps."
    - "Stir-fry rice 3-4 minutes, pressing it against the wok for char."
    - "Add peas, carrots, and soy sauce. Toss vigorously."
    - "Return scrambled eggs. Add sesame oil and green onions. Toss once more."
    - "Season with white pepper and serve immediately."
