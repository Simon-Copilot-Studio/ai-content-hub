  ingredients:
    - "6 egg yolks"
    - "3/4 cup sugar"
    - "1 1/4 cups mascarpone cheese"
    - "2 cups heavy cream"
    - "2 cups strong espresso, cooled"
    - "3 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)"
    - "1 package ladyfinger biscuits"
    - "Cocoa powder for dusting"
  steps:
    - "Beat egg yolks and sugar until thick and pale, about 5 minutes."
    - "Add mascarpone and mix until smooth."
    - "In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream to stiff peaks. Fold into mascarpone mixture."
    - "Combine espresso and coffee liqueur. Quickly dip each ladyfinger — don't soak."
    - "Layer dipped ladyfingers in a 9x13 dish. Spread half the cream on top."
    - "Repeat layers. Refrigerate at least 6 hours or overnight."
    - "Dust generously with cocoa powder just before serving."
