  ingredients:
    - "6 eggs"
    - "1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes"
    - "1 red bell pepper, diced"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "4 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "2 tsp cumin"
    - "2 tsp paprika"
    - "1 tsp chili flakes"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "Fresh cilantro and crumbled feta for garnish"
    - "Crusty bread for serving"
  steps:
    - "Heat olive oil in a large skillet. Sauté onion and bell pepper until softened."
    - "Add garlic, cumin, paprika, and chili flakes. Cook 1 minute until fragrant."
    - "Pour in crushed tomatoes. Simmer 10 minutes until sauce thickens."
    - "Make 6 wells in the sauce. Crack an egg into each well."
    - "Cover and cook 5-8 minutes until egg whites are set but yolks are still runny."
    - "Garnish with cilantro and feta. Serve with crusty bread for dipping."
