  ingredients:
    - "2 cups sushi rice"
    - "2 1/2 cups water"
    - "3 tbsp rice vinegar"
    - "1 tbsp sugar"
    - "8 oz imitation crab (or real crab)"
    - "1 avocado, sliced"
    - "1 cucumber, julienned"
    - "Nori sheets"
    - "Sesame seeds"
    - "Soy sauce, wasabi, pickled ginger for serving"
  steps:
    - "Cook sushi rice. Season with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt while still warm. Let cool."
    - "Place nori on a bamboo mat. Spread rice evenly over nori, leaving 1 inch at the top."
    - "Flip so nori is on top (inside-out roll). Sprinkle rice with sesame seeds."
    - "Place crab, avocado, and cucumber in a line across the center."
    - "Using the mat, roll tightly away from you. Squeeze gently to shape."
    - "Slice with a wet knife into 8 pieces. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger."
