  ingredients:
    - "1/2 cup butter"
    - "1 cup sugar"
    - "2 eggs"
    - "1 tsp vanilla"
    - "1/3 cup cocoa powder"
    - "1/2 cup flour"
    - "1/4 tsp salt"
    - "1/4 tsp baking powder"
    - "1/2 cup chocolate chips"
  steps:
    - "Melt butter in a saucepan. Remove from heat and stir in sugar."
    - "Add eggs and vanilla, stir until smooth."
    - "Mix in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder until just combined."
    - "Fold in chocolate chips. Pour into a greased 8x8 pan."
    - "Bake at 350°F for 22-25 minutes. The center should be slightly underset — it firms up as it cools."
    - "Cool completely in pan before cutting for the cleanest squares."
