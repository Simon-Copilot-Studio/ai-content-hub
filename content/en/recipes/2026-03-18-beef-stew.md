  ingredients:
    - "2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1.5-inch cubes"
    - "4 medium potatoes, cubed"
    - "3 large carrots, sliced"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "4 cups beef broth"
    - "1 cup red wine"
    - "2 tbsp tomato paste"
    - "2 tbsp flour"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "2 sprigs fresh thyme"
    - "2 bay leaves"
    - "Salt and pepper to taste"
  steps:
    - "Season beef with salt and pepper. Toss with flour. Brown in batches in olive oil in a Dutch oven. Set aside."
    - "Sauté onion in the same pot until softened. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 1 minute."
    - "Deglaze with red wine, scraping up browned bits."
    - "Return beef to pot. Add broth, thyme, and bay leaves. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer."
    - "Cover and cook for 1 hour. Add potatoes and carrots, continue cooking 45-60 minutes until everything is tender."
    - "Remove bay leaves and thyme. Adjust seasoning and serve with crusty bread."
