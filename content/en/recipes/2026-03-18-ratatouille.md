    - "2 zucchini, thinly sliced"
    - "2 yellow squash, thinly sliced"
    - "2 Roma tomatoes, thinly sliced"
    - "1 eggplant, thinly sliced"
    - "1 bell pepper, diced"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes"
    - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "Fresh thyme and basil"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Make sauce: sauté onion and bell pepper until soft. Add garlic and crushed tomatoes. Season and spread in a baking dish."
    - "Arrange alternating slices of zucchini, squash, tomato, and eggplant in a spiral pattern over the sauce."
    - "Drizzle with olive oil. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh thyme."
    - "Cover tightly with foil. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes."
    - "Remove foil. Bake 15 more minutes until vegetables are tender and edges are slightly caramelized."
    - "Garnish with fresh basil. Serve as a main course with crusty bread or as a side dish."
