  ingredients:
    - "1.5 cups green or brown lentils, rinsed"
    - "1 onion, diced"
    - "2 carrots, diced"
    - "2 celery stalks, diced"
    - "3 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes"
    - "6 cups vegetable broth"
    - "1 tsp cumin"
    - "1/2 tsp smoked paprika"
    - "2 cups fresh spinach"
    - "Lemon wedges for serving"
  steps:
    - "Sauté onion, carrots, and celery in olive oil until softened, about 5 minutes."
    - "Add garlic, cumin, and paprika. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant."
    - "Add lentils, diced tomatoes, and broth. Bring to a boil."
    - "Reduce heat and simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender."
    - "Stir in spinach until wilted. Season with salt and pepper."
    - "Serve with a squeeze of lemon — it brightens everything."
