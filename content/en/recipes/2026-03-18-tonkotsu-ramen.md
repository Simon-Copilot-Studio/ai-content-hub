  ingredients:
    - "Broth: 3 lbs pork bones, 1 lb pork fatback, 6 cloves garlic, 2-inch ginger"
    - "Chashu: 1 lb pork belly, 1/2 cup soy sauce, 1/2 cup mirin, 1/4 cup sugar"
    - "4 portions fresh ramen noodles"
    - "4 soft-boiled eggs (6.5 minutes)"
    - "Toppings: corn, nori, green onions, sesame seeds, chili oil"
  steps:
    - "Blanch pork bones in boiling water 10 minutes. Drain, rinse, and scrub clean."
    - "Add bones, fatback, garlic, and ginger to a fresh pot of water. Bring to a rolling boil."
    - "Maintain a vigorous boil (not simmer!) for 10-12 hours, adding water as needed. The broth turns milky white."
    - "Make chashu: roll and tie pork belly. Braise in soy sauce, mirin, and sugar for 2-3 hours until tender. Slice."
    - "Strain broth. Season with salt and soy sauce to taste."
    - "Cook ramen noodles according to package. Place in bowls."
    - "Ladle hot broth over noodles. Top with chashu, soft-boiled egg, corn, nori, and green onions."
