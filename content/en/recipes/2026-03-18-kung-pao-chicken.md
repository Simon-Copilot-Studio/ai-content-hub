  prepTime: "15M"

  calories: "380"
  category: "Main Course"
  cuisine: "Chinese"
  ingredients:
    - "1 lb chicken thigh, diced"
    - "1/2 cup roasted peanuts"
    - "8-10 dried red chilies"
    - "1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns"
    - "3 green onions, chopped"
    - "Sauce: 2 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp hoisin, 1 tsp sugar, 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water"
    - "2 cloves garlic, minced"
    - "1 tbsp ginger, minced"
    - "2 tbsp vegetable oil"
  steps:
    - "Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl. Set aside."
    - "Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add dried chilies and Sichuan peppercorns, fry 30 seconds until fragrant."
    - "Add chicken. Stir-fry until golden and cooked through, about 5 minutes."
    - "Add garlic and ginger. Cook 30 seconds."
    - "Pour in sauce. Stir until it thickens and coats the chicken."
    - "Toss in peanuts and green onions. Serve immediately over steamed rice."
