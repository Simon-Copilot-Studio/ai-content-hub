  ingredients:
    - "2 cups heavy cream"
    - "1 cup whole milk"
    - "3/4 cup sugar"
    - "5 egg yolks"
    - "1 vanilla bean (or 2 tsp pure vanilla extract)"
    - "Pinch of salt"
  steps:
    - "Heat cream, milk, and half the sugar in a saucepan. Split vanilla bean and scrape seeds into the mixture."
    - "Whisk egg yolks with remaining sugar until pale."
    - "Slowly pour hot cream into yolks while whisking (tempering). Return to pan."
    - "Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture coats the back of a spoon (170°F)."
    - "Strain through a fine mesh sieve. Chill completely in an ice bath, then refrigerate overnight."
    - "Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer instructions. Transfer to a container and freeze until firm."
