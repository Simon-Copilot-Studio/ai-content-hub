    - "2 cups Greek yogurt (full-fat)"
    - "1 English cucumber, grated and squeezed dry"
    - "2 cloves garlic, finely grated"
    - "2 tbsp olive oil"
    - "1 tbsp lemon juice"
    - "1 tbsp fresh dill, chopped"
    - "Salt and pepper"
  steps:
    - "Grate cucumber and squeeze out as much liquid as possible — this is crucial for thick tzatziki."
    - "Combine yogurt, cucumber, garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, dill, salt, and pepper."
    - "Stir well. Taste and adjust seasoning."
    - "Refrigerate at least 1 hour for flavors to meld."
    - "Drizzle with olive oil before serving. Perfect with pita, grilled chicken, or falafel."
