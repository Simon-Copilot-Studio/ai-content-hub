    - "1 lb lump crab meat"
    - "1/3 cup breadcrumbs"
    - "1 egg"
    - "2 tbsp mayonnaise"
    - "1 tbsp Dijon mustard"
    - "1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce"
    - "1 tsp Old Bay seasoning"
    - "2 tbsp fresh parsley"
    - "Butter for pan-frying"
    - "Remoulade sauce for serving"
  steps:
    - "Pick through crab meat to remove any shell pieces."
    - "Gently mix crab with breadcrumbs, egg, mayo, Dijon, Worcestershire, Old Bay, and parsley. Handle gently to keep lumps intact."
    - "Form into 4-6 patties. Refrigerate 30 minutes."
    - "Pan-fry in butter over medium-high heat, 3-4 minutes per side until golden."
    - "Serve with remoulade sauce and lemon wedges."
