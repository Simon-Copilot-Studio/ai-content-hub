    - "4 large eggs"
    - "2 English muffins, split and toasted"
    - "4 slices Canadian bacon"
    - "3 egg yolks (for hollandaise)"
    - "1/2 cup melted butter"
    - "1 tbsp lemon juice"
    - "1 tsp white vinegar (for poaching)"
    - "Pinch of cayenne pepper"
    - "Fresh chives for garnish"
  steps:
    - "Make hollandaise: Whisk egg yolks and lemon juice over a double boiler until thickened. Slowly drizzle in melted butter while whisking constantly. Season with cayenne and salt."
    - "Bring a pot of water to a gentle simmer. Add vinegar. Create a whirlpool and slide eggs in one at a time. Poach 3-4 minutes."
    - "Toast English muffins and warm Canadian bacon in a skillet."
    - "Assemble: muffin, bacon, poached egg, hollandaise. Garnish with chives and cracked pepper."
